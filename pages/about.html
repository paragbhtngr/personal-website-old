Hi! Thanks for checking out my website :D

I'm Parag Bhatnagar - a Yale-NUS Computer Science Major with an interest in pretty, functional things. I like to build stuff for the web and have a love-hate relationship with CSS and a mostly love relationship with Webpack. I dabble in digital art, graphic design and the occasional 3D project. You can check out a resume of my work here.

I have a fondness for accents, a love of movies and an obsession with stand-up comedy. I'm a strong believer in the artistic value of graphic novels (seriously - Alex Ross' paintings are worthy of any gallery) and a fervent opponent of pretension and ego in the design process.

This site is currently a work-in-progress, so don't panic if something goes wrong. Feel free to apply aformentioned philosophy to your life, as you see fit.
